## The Philosophy: Respecting the Distance

Endurance running and cycling are not casual hobbies; they are a fundamental practice in compounding consistency. Over the last 20 years on the roads, the lesson has remained identical to my academic belief: **you do not sprint a marathon, and you do not sprint through a rigorous mathematical foundation.** You choose an intentional pace, embrace the resistance, and respect the distance. The discipline required to wake up at dawn and log miles over decades is the exact same discipline required to master a complex engineering domain.
