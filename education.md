## The Philosophy: Domain Thinking Over Rote Memory

Rote memorization is dead; 2026 has officially proven it to fail. When an AI pipeline can generate code, mathematical equations, and localized simulations in seconds, a student's competitive edge is no longer raw retention. It is the ability to look at a complex system, trace it back to basic calculus or physics, and judge if the automated tool is innovating or hallucinating.

Because of this, my teaching methodology rejects the standard race to "complete the syllabus." We do not rush to check institutional boxes, and we do not participate in high-stress weekly testing cycles that prioritize short-term memory over deep structural intuition. We take the time required to build an unbreakable foundation.
